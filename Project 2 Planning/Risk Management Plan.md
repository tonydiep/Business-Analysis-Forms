- _2-Low: there is a 20–40% probability that the event will occur_
- _3-Medium: there is a 40–60% probability that the event will occur_

## Definitions of impact by objective

_Document how impact will be measured and defined for either the project as a whole or for each objective. Include the scale used and the definition for each level in the impact scale. For example:_

### Cost impacts:

- _1-Very low: budget overrun on control account <5%_
- _2-Low: budget overrun on control account between 5–10%_
- _3-Medium: budget overrun on control account between 10–15%_
- _4-High: budget overrun on control account between 15–20%_
- _5-Very high: budget overrun on control account of >20%_

### Scope

- 1-Very Low: _Minimal deviation from requirements_
- 2-Low: _The product is deficient in a few minor requirements_
- 3-Medium: _The product is deficient in one major requirement or multiple minor requirements_
- 4-High: _The product is deficient in multiple essential requirements_
- 5-Very High: _The product does not meet the objectives and is effectively useless_

### Quality

- 1-Very Low: _Minimal deviation in performance_
- 2-Low: _There is minor deviation in performance_
- 3-Medium: _At least one performance requirement is significantly deficient_
- 4-High: _Major aspects of performance do not meet requirements_
- 5-Very High: _Performance is significantly below objectives and is effective useless_

### Schedule

- 1-Very Low: _Slippage on noncritical paths but float remains_
- 2-Low: _Noncritical paths have used all their float, or overall schedule increase of 1% to 5%_
- 3-Medium: _Between 5% and 10% overall schedule increase_
- 4-High: _Between 10% and 20% overall schedule increase_
- 5-Very High: _Greater than 20% overall schedule increase_

### Cost

- 1-Very Low: _Cost increase that requires use of some contingency but some contingency funds remain_
- 2-Low: _Cost increase that requires use of all contingency funds_
- 3-Medium: _Cost increase of 5% to 10%_
- 4-High: _Cost increase of 10% to 20%_
- 5-Very High: _Cost increase of greater than 20%_
